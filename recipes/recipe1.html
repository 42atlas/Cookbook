Crescent Breakfast Tart


    INGREDIENTS


  • - 1 can crescent dough
  • - 6 eggs
  • - 2/3 c. white cheddar
  • - 4 slices cooked bacon
  • - kosher salt
  • - Freshly ground black pepper
  • - 1 tbsp. Chopped chives, for garnish

  • DIRECTIONS


  • 1st: Preheat oven to 400Â° and line a medium baking sheet with parchment paper. Place crescent dough on baking sheet and pinch seams together.

  • 2nd: Fold edges of dough in to create a crust. Crack eggs onto crescent dough and sprinkle with cheese. Place bacon on top and season all over with salt and pepper.

  • 3rd: Carefully transfer baking sheet to oven and bake until crust is golden and egg whites are set, 10 to 15 minutes.

  • 4th: Garnish with chives. Slice into squares and serve warm.
